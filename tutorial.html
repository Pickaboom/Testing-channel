RESOURCES TUTORIAL

How to use the resources board

This quick walkthrough shows people how to explore the page, move around, zoom in, and understand what they are looking at.

What this page is for

This page is a visual board for Pickaboom resources, guides, hosting info, event ideas, rules, and connected topics.

Use it to read information instead of searching through lots of messages.
Related ideas are grouped together to make things easier to understand.

How to move around

The board can be larger than your screen, so you will need to pan around to explore it.

Middle mouse drag = move around the board
Space + drag = another way to pan
Normal scrolling also helps move through the page

How to zoom

You can zoom in to read details and zoom out to understand the bigger layout.

Ctrl/Cmd + mouse wheel = zoom
Press + or - to zoom with keys
Press 0 to reset and F to fit the full board

Frames and sections

Big boxes help organise the board so you know where each topic starts and what belongs together.

Frames = big topic areas
Sections = smaller grouped parts inside a topic

Nodes are the main info cards

Nodes usually contain the actual information you want to read, like steps, notes, rules, or tips.

Read titles first to find what you need faster
Then open your focus on the description text inside each card

Lines show related topics

Connections between cards help show flow, related ideas, next steps, or linked information.

If two cards are linked, they probably belong to the same topic or path
Follow lines when you want the “next part” of something
Most users are here to view and learn, while admins may manage the layout
